# Twilio A2P 10DLC Campaign Registration

## Campaign Use Case
**Account Notifications**

## Campaign Description
EliteRank is a social competition platform where users are nominated and voted on as "Most Eligible" in their city. This campaign sends transactional SMS notifications to users about their account activity including: new votes received, nomination invitations, competition round advancement, voting window open/close reminders, and winner announcements. All messages are triggered by platform events, not marketing.

## Sample Message #1
EliteRank: You just received 5 new votes in Most Eligible Dallas 2026! You're now ranked #3. View your standings: https://eliterank.co/profile. Reply STOP to unsubscribe.

## Sample Message #2
EliteRank: You've been nominated for Most Eligible NYC 2026! Accept your nomination by Mar 15 to compete: https://eliterank.co/claim/abc123. Msg & data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out.

## Sample Message #3
EliteRank: Voting is now open for Most Eligible Miami 2026! Cast your votes before Mar 20: https://eliterank.co/vote. Reply HELP for help or STOP to unsubscribe.

## Message Contents Checkboxes
- [x] Messages will include embedded links
- [ ] Messages will include phone numbers
- [ ] Direct lending content
- [ ] Age-gated content

## How do end-users consent to receive messages?
End users consent to receive SMS notifications from EliteRank during account registration at https://eliterank.co. During sign-up, users provide their phone number and must manually check an unchecked consent checkbox that reads: "I agree to receive SMS notifications from EliteRank about votes, nominations, competition updates, and results. Message frequency varies. Msg & data rates may apply. Reply STOP to unsubscribe." The checkbox is not pre-checked and is not required to complete registration. Users can also enable SMS notifications later through their account settings at https://eliterank.co/settings. Consent records including timestamp and the specific consent language are stored in our database. Our privacy policy at https://eliterank.co/privacy includes full SMS disclosure.
